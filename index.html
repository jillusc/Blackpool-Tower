An introduction to what's worth knowing about this iconic northern landmark

The Blackpool Tower

Standing tall on the central promenade since 1894, the design of this no. 1 attraction was undeniably inspired by the Eiffel Tower in Paris. Today, Blackpool Tower welcomes thousands of visitors each year to the archetypal northern English seaside town.

It is home to the grand Tower Ballroom which boasts a real Wurlitzer organ and a rich history of hosting dance shows.

At ground level, you can find the Tower Circus where a live band provides the shows' dynamic musical accompaniment.

Another top attraction here is the Blackpool Tower Dungeon experience hosted by Merlin Entertainment, featuring a dungeon-themed Escape Room.
